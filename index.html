ABOUT LogicaLaw

We are a Cyprus-based Law Firm that believes in doing things differently.

Our partners will always be on hand to deliver personal attention

By operating a Chambers-model, we recruit Partners with expertise in their practice areas, and allow them to focus on delivering value to their clients. We get to know you and your business, and by dealing directly and not through junior lawyers, we increase the value offered to you.

We provide tailored advice to our clients.

Using our firm’s unique model we apply the skills we have to advising a wide variety of clients, from startups to multinationals. Without the hierarchical structure found in most law firms our Partners are free to give the time and focus you need, without having to feed the top of a pyramid or follow bureaucratic rules.

No hourly bills. No bull.

We believe in flexible fee arrangements including capped and fixed structures. This provides clients with certainty and clarity for their engagements.

Our Partners focus on what is truly important – commercially relevant advice delivered efficiently.

SERVICES

We at LogicaLaw believe that people should focus on what they are good at. By recruiting experienced Partners we can manage core commercial work and complex projects. We also believe that most legal work can be done better by a familiar team of partners without delegation.

Our team currently has expertise in the following practice areas:

General Corporate

We can assist our clients on general matters such as the incorporation of companies in Cyprus or other jurisdictions as well as the management of these. We also provide advice on a wide range of corporate matters such as changes to ownership/management and ordinary trading, restructuring and financing. We can also advise on Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP’s) and related agreements and legislation. We have extensive experience with shareholder agreements and disputes, joint ventures, debt and security arrangements, share re-organisations and buybacks and corporate governance and directors’ duties.

Mergers and acquisitions

We can advise on the sale and purchase of businesses, shares or assets in a wide range of sectors. We can get involved at any stage in the deal process: from heads of terms through due diligence, contractual negotiation and drafting, dealing with financing/security issues and completion. We can review and advise on the level of contractual protection that would be appropriate given the size of the transaction. We aim to keep costs to a reasonable level while properly managing risk.

Private equity/venture capital

We can advise on the full spectrum of private equity and venture capital work, from seed and series A investment rounds, growth equity investments and new acquisitions through to portfolio company exits. We act for sponsors, growth companies and management teams and seek to add value in the life cycle of the growth company from inception to exit.

We can advise our clients on a wide range of legal and practical issues arising from the operation and development of their businesses. These include implementation of major projects, managing existing relationships and the formation of new ones.

We are happy to be involved in every aspect of our clients’ commercial affairs and seek to act as an integral part of their organization as part of a part-time General Counsel service.
